Nick is a highly sought speaker across the nation. He speaks predominately to multiple law enforcement agencies as well as schools, corporations, and churches. He serves his community as a Mentor/Instructor at the Midwest Marines Focus Program. He is currently pursuing a Master’s Degree in Education. Nick was the GOP nominee for Congress in 2010 for California’s 51st District. His drive to serve our nation continues as he is now a candidate for the United States House of Representatives in California’s 53rd Congressional District.
