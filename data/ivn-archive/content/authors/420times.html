The 420 TIMES is a Los Angeles-based medical marijuana and natural healing magazine, distributed throughout Southern California. The 420 Times covers activism, advocacy and legal issues, cultivation, medical research, as well as news and feature stories. The 420 Times is also available online at www.the420times.com, along with Facebook, Twitter and via the popular weekly e-mail update, The Smoke Signal. The 420 Times has been nominated for Los Angeles Press Club awards in journalism and been referenced by both local and national media outlets for feature stories and breaking news on medical marijuana. Recent interviews have included: former Mexico President Vicente Fox, Snoop Dogg, Wiz Khalifa, Cheech & Chong, Andrew Sullivan, Larry Flynt and presidential candidate Gary Johnson.
