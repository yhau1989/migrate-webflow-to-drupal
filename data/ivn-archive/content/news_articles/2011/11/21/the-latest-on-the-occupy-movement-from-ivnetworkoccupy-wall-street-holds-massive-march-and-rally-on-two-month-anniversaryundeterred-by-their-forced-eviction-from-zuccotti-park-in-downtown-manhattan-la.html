Occupy Wall Street holds massive march and rally on two-month anniversary

Undeterred by their forced eviction from Zuccotti Park in downtown Manhattan last Tuesday, Occupy Wall Street protesters commemorated the two-month anniversary of the ongoing demonstration with a day of action that culminated in a massive rally and march across the Brooklyn Bridge on Thursday, November 17th.
