The Google Crisis Response Team provides a shareable and highly detailed map of Hurricane Sandy. Google Crisis Response has been making life saving information available on the Internet since Hurricane Katrina in 2005. Some of the critical features that are made available through the map are Evacuation Routes, Webcams, Traffic Conditions, and Surge Probabilities.

You can share this map on Google+, Twitter, Facebook, or embed it by clicking the 'Share' button.
