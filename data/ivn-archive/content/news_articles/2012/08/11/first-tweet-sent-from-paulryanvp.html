Minutes before a Saturday announcement, an official @PaulRyanVP account tweeted out a link touting the Romney-Ryan ticket as "America's Comeback Team".

The tweet links to a campaign donation page. "We are proud to announce Paul Ryan as Mitt's VP. Together we will offer a plan to restore American greatness and help build a stronger middle class," it says.
