American cinema has delivered audiences many unforgettable moments. Throughout the decades a number of films embodying American patriotism and inherent American values have been box office hits nationwide. Our list of firecracker film moments represents a number of patriotic values. Some sadly remind us of where our country used to be, while others show how far this country has come. A few are wildly fictional while several story lines closely resemble our nation’s history. Regardless, each symbolizes the importance of national unity and reminds us this country’s wins, losses, and great accomplishments.
