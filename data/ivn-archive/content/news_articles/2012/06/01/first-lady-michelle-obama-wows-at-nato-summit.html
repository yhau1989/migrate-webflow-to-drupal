Would like to give Michelle Obama a slow clap for her sartorial choice at the recent NATO Summit held in Chicago. As style blog Mrs. O reports, she wore an inspired sheath dress and blazer combination by Zac Posen, with a modern cut and interesting neckline. The fit was perfect for the First Lady. It is always refreshing to see contemporary, non-boring suits worn on high-profile professional women.
