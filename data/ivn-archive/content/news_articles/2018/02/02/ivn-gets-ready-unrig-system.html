An unprecedented gathering of the brightest minds from the right and left with one common purpose: fix American politics. Leaders in advocacy, policy, politics, legal, movement-building, and popular culture share their knowledge and experience to build lasting solutions that inspire change.

The Unrig the System Summit will be fast-paced and fun, with time to mingle with top advocacy leaders, academics, comedians, musicians, celebrities, activists, philanthropists and journalists, as well as plenty of New Orleans fun mixed in.

This summit will be like no other, and IVN is excited to say we'll be in attendance to cover the event!

There will be discussions and panels across partisan and ideological divides focused on working together to unrig America's political system.
