On April 26, 2017, Centrist Project Executive Director Nick Troiano sat down with radio host Michael Smerconish to give his take on the growing independent movement.

Nick discussed the hurdles he faced as an independent candidate for the U.S House, the need for centrist leadership in government now, and how The Centrist Project is working to help elect that type of leadership.
